🌱 COMMUNITY OUTREACH PROGRAM

Outreach 2025

📅 DECEMBER 27, 2025
📍 BARANGAY 10, CALOOCAN CITY
💚 HEART FOR THE NATION PH
🌱
VIEW FULL PHOTO

Isang araw ng love, joy, at hope ang hatid ng Outreach Program sa Barangay 10, Caloocan City noong December 27, 2025. A day filled with smiles, warmth, and the reminder that technology is meaningless without humanity — and that community is everything.

Led by Heart for the Nation — Philippines, founded by Mr. Lemuel Peurtollano and Ms. Claire Trono, this outreach was organized in collaboration with ENSC, CASSC, LITS, and DCSS. Under the leadership of Chairman Mr. Emmanuel Forfieda, tuloy-tuloy ang mission ng organization mula outreach programs hanggang sa pagbibigay ng scholarships para sa underprivileged youth.

THE PEOPLE BEHIND IT

Heart for the Nation — Philippines was founded with a single goal: to reach those who need it most. Under the visionary leadership of Chairman Mr. Emmanuel Forfieda, the organization has grown from outreach programs to providing full scholarships for underprivileged youth. Founders Mr. Lemuel Peurtollano and Ms. Claire Trono built an institution of service — and on December 27, they invited LITS to be part of it.

"Isang araw ng love, joy, at hope ang hatid ng Outreach Program sa Barangay 10, Caloocan City. Thank you to all volunteers and partners for spreading hope and kindness!"

— LITS Official · #LITS2526

WHAT HAPPENED

The kids of Barangay 10 received food and brand new toys — simple gifts that meant the world. Volunteers from LITS, ENSC, CASSC, and DCSS spent the day with the community, playing with children, distributing relief goods, and sharing a moment of genuine human connection that no technology can replicate.

We hope everyone had a great time, especially the kids. This is what LITS means when it says it builds more than just technical professionals — it builds people of character.

PHOTO GALLERY

// WHAT HEART FOR THE NATION PH DOES

Beyond this single outreach day, Heart for the Nation — Philippines runs ongoing programs supporting underprivileged communities across the region. Their scholarship program has opened the doors of education for countless youth who would otherwise have been left behind. When LITS partners with organizations like this, it proves that IT students are not just future technologists — they are future leaders with hearts for their communities.
