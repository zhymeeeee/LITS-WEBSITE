🌻 GENERAL ASSEMBLY RECAP

GA 2025

📅 SEPTEMBER 16, 2025
⏰ 1:00 PM – 5:00 PM
📍 Engineering Building, MPH1
🌻
VIEW FULL PHOTO

Wabby Wabbo! The LITS General Assembly 2025 was not your typical org meeting. With a theme built around growth, community, and a healthy amount of zombie-themed urgency, this year's GA set the tone for what LITS 2025–2026 is going to be: bold, warm, and absolutely unforgettable.

The Engineering Building's MPH1 came alive on September 16 as both new seedlings — first-time members stepping into the LITS community — and strong sunflowers — returning members who've seen the org grow season after season — gathered under one roof to celebrate, learn, and connect.

READY, SET, PLANT!

The program opened with the story of how LITS first started. For the new members, it was an introduction to a legacy. For the older members, it was a reminder of what they're part of. Through unity and dedication, LITS has grown into a thriving community of IT students who genuinely care about each other's success — and the General Assembly was the annual moment to reaffirm that commitment.

The afternoon was packed with games that brought the entire room together — the kind of chaotic, fun, competitive energy that only happens when you get a room full of IT students in one place and give them something to compete for. Prizes were won, alliances were formed, and more than a few friendships were forged in the heat of battle.
