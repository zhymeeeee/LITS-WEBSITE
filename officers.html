// SENIOR ADVISER — LITS, SINCE 2004
Dr. Froilan De Guzman
DEPARTMENT HEAD OF COMPUTER STUDIES · NEARLY 20 YEARS OF SERVICE

Nearly Two Decades at the Helm

Dr. Froilan De Guzman has served as LITS Faculty Adviser since the organization's founding in 2004 — making him the longest-serving adviser in LITS history. His commitment to IT students has never wavered across nearly 20 years and countless generations of officer changes.

The Teacher Behind the Title

He is a truly admirable instructor who possesses not just great knowledge, but the wits to go with it — and the perfect amount of humor that makes every class feel like a privilege. His lessons are like a gift wrapped in gold — ones his students carry long after they graduate.

Surviving Dissolution

LITS was dissolved at one point during its early years, and Dr. De Guzman was initially reluctant to continue as adviser. But a persistent group of lower-year students convinced him that the organization was worth reviving. He returned — and from that revival forward, LITS never stopped growing.

A Legacy of People

He has shaped not only LITS, but the lives of countless IT students at UE Caloocan. As LITS adviser, he has always wanted only what is best for the organization — and as a mentor, only what is best for every student. He is, simply, a one-of-a-kind person inside and outside the classroom.

Prof. Ryan Celis
INSTRUCTOR I · BSIT DEPARTMENT

That One Professor

Prof. Ryan Celis is that one professor who never gets tired of teaching — someone who makes everything easier to understand and whose patience students truly appreciate. His way of breaking down complex IT concepts into their simplest, most enjoyable forms is something his students never forget.

More Than Technical

He teaches more than technology. He teaches the attitudes and values that are vital for a future in IT — and he does it with kindness, dedication, and a commitment to his students that goes far beyond the classroom. His lectures are, quite simply, legendary.

Role in LITS

As LITS Junior Adviser, Prof. Celis serves as technical consultant and genuine supporter of everything the organization stands for, consistently inspiring members to become better versions of themselves.

// PRESIDENT — LITS A.Y. 2025–2026
Nicole David
PRESIDENT · BSIT — 4TH YEAR

A consistent academic achiever and scholar, Nicole David is a 4th year BSIT student who has grown with LITS every step of the way. She served as LITS Secretary during A.Y. 2024–2025, and now leads the organization as President for 2025–2026. Her journey from secretary to president gives her a unique, behind-the-scenes understanding of the org's heartbeat — combined with a deep commitment to every IT student's welfare.

Nicole's platform rests on Technical Empowerment, Inclusive Community, and Industry Readiness. Through these pillars she aims to bridge academic excellence with real-world skills, ensuring that every member grows both personally and professionally.

"LITS is not just a line on your resume — it's the community that shapes who you become as a professional. Whether you're a freshman or a senior, there's always room to learn, lead, and belong."

// VICE PRESIDENT — EXTERNAL — LITS A.Y. 2025–2026
Charlize Fe
VP — EXTERNAL · BSIT — 4TH YEAR

A naturally hardworking and driven leader, Charlize Fe brings extensive governance experience to LITS. Before joining the organization, she served as the Engineering Student Council President from 2023 to 2024, where she honed her skills in representation and stakeholder management. Now as LITS Vice President for External Affairs for the 2025–2026 term, she channels that same dedication toward building bridges between IT students and the broader professional community.

Charlize manages all inter-organizational relationships, industry partnerships, media presence, and public-facing events as LITS's external ambassador. Her leadership has already strengthened multiple industry collaborations and opened new channels for exclusive member opportunities, ensuring that LITS remains a vital link between academic life and career readiness.

She believes that strong external connections create internal growth. Through strategic partnerships and authentic engagement, Charlize aims to give every LITS member access to mentors, companies, and real-world experiences.
