Curiosity and critical thinking took center stage as we explored cybersecurity through a CTF-based learning experience. Participants were exposed to real-world attack scenarios and practical security concepts, learning not just how exploits work, but how to defend against them.

"Learn the skills and hack the right way — ethical hacking isn't about breaking things, it's about understanding how to protect them."

— Ray Vincent Concepcion

WHAT YOU'LL LEARN

This online seminar will cover the fundamentals of ethical hacking through a Capture The Flag (CTF) approach. Topics include:

  • Web exploitation techniques (SQLi, XSS, CSRF)
  • Cryptography basics and hash cracking
  • Reverse engineering fundamentals
  • Privilege escalation concepts
  • CTF competition strategies

WHY ATTEND?

Learn from someone who has proven their skills on both national and international stages. Vince's competitive experience brings real-world insights that you won't get from textbooks. Whether you're completely new to cybersecurity or looking to level up your CTF game, this workshop has something for you.
