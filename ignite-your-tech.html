🔥 SEMINAR RECAP

IGNITE YOUR TECH JOURNEY

📅 SEPTEMBER 9, 2025
🏛️ LEAGUE OF INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY STUDENTS
📣 LITS 2025–2026
🔥
VIEW FULL PHOTO

Ready to spark your future in tech? IgnITe Your Tech Journey was a seminar designed for IT students who are hungry to know what the industry really looks like — the wins, the grind, and the habits that separate those who make it from those who don't. And on September 9, 2025, LITS delivered exactly that.

THE SPEAKERS

Two industry voices. Two stories. One afternoon that reminded every student in the room why they chose IT in the first place.

👩‍💻
Ms. Allyza Denisse Bispo
// INDUSTRY SPEAKER
Ms. Bispo shared her personal journey through the IT landscape — the decisions that shaped her career, the moments of doubt, and the mindset shifts that made the difference. She spoke with the kind of honesty that only someone who's lived through it can offer.
👨‍💻
Mr. Kurt Carvey Cadenas
// INDUSTRY SPEAKER
Mr. Cadenas brought practical, actionable insights about building the right habits and foundation for a career in tech. His talk challenged students to think beyond grades — to think about who they're becoming as professionals.

THE TAKEAWAY

IT isn't just about codes and programs. Tuesday's talk was a clear reminder that the industry rewards people who build the right habits, stay curious, and commit to continuous improvement. A 1% improvement every day builds the strongest foundations — and that's exactly the energy LITS wants every member to carry.

"A 1% improvement daily builds the strongest foundations."

— #1%Daily #IgnITeYourTechJourney

The seminar wasn't just a talk — it was a conversation. Students came with questions about career paths, specializations, and what the industry actually needs from fresh graduates. The speakers didn't hold back, and the room was all the better for it. By the time the session wrapped, the energy in the room was unmistakably different — sharper, more intentional, more focused on what comes next.
