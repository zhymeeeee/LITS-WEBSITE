WHAT YOU'LL LEARN

This hands-on workshop will explore how to integrate AI agents into modern development workflows. Learn to use cutting-edge tools and frameworks that are reshaping the way we build, automate, and innovate. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced developer, this session will give you practical skills you can apply immediately.

"Make Each kWh Count — transforming how energy is managed, distributed, and consumed through smarter, data-driven energy use."

— ElectrifAI PH Mission

WHY ATTEND?

AI is no longer the future — it's the present. Developers who understand how to work with AI agents will have a significant advantage in the job market. This workshop gives you practical, hands-on experience that you can add to your portfolio and resume.

Plus, you'll get to network with fellow IT students and learn from someone who's actually building AI solutions for the Philippine market and representing the country on international stages through the Ethereum Foundation.
