ABOUT THE ORGANIZATION

Who is LITS?

The LEAGUE OF INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY STUDENTS (LITS) is a dynamic, forward-thinking student organization dedicated to empowering IT students through meaningful programs, skill-building workshops, and a tight-knit community of passionate tech enthusiasts.

Founded on the belief that technology can transform lives, LITS bridges the gap between academic learning and real-world application — hosting events, seminars, and competitions that challenge members to think critically and innovate boldly.

Whether you're a first-year student finding your footing or a senior developer ready to give back — LITS is your home. We cultivate leaders, nurture talent, and build a generation of IT professionals ready to shape the digital world.
