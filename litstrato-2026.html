LITSTRATO 2025

📅 A.Y. 2025–2026
📸 PHOTOGRAPHY COMPETITION
🏆 WINNERS REVEALED
📸
VIEW FULL PHOTO

ITuloy ang Kwento. Buhayin ang Alaala. LITSTRATO 2025 was more than a photography contest — it was an invitation to every IT student to see their university with new eyes, to pick up a camera, and to say: this moment matters, this place has a story, and I am the one who will tell it.

The challenge was simple but profound: capture through your lens the memories, the legacy, and the spirit of Silangan that continue to live in every frame. Tales of triumph, struggles, and inspiration — frozen in time for everyone who came after to witness.

THE CHALLENGE

"Handa ka na bang ibahagi ang kwentong Silangan na tumatak sa iyong puso? Let's continue the story together — because here, every shot speaks volumes."

— LITSTRATO 2025 Official Call for Entries

LITSTRATO 2025 called on participants to go beyond the obvious, beyond the postcard-perfect shot, and find the frames that carry real weight — the candid moments between classes, the worn edges of a beloved building, the faces of students who pour everything into what they do. Photography as documentation. Photography as memory. Photography as legacy.

// A NOTE ON STORYTELLING

Every great organization has stories worth telling. LITSTRATO 2025 was LITS' way of saying: we trust you to tell ours. The university is full of moments that happen once, in a flash, and then they're gone — unless someone is there to catch them. This competition was about building that habit of looking, of noticing, of caring enough to press the shutter.

To everyone who participated — you helped continue the kwento. And to the winners, your frames will be part of LITS history now. Thank you for seeing what others might have walked past.
