CTRL + PLAY

📅 OCTOBER 9–10, 2025
🎮 MOBILE LEGENDS & VALORANT
🏆 LITS ESPORTS 2025
🎮
VIEW FULL PHOTO

IT Warriors, the arena was set — and the battle was real. CTRL + PLAY: LITS Esports 2025 brought the fire, the competition, and the unforgettable moments that only happen when gamers who live and breathe their craft go head to head. Two days. Two games. One championship.

From registration all the way to the final match, CTRL + PLAY was proof that LITS isn't just about seminars and assemblies — it's about celebrating every dimension of IT culture, including the one that keeps a lot of us up until 3AM.

REGISTRATION DETAILS

The tournament was open to all IT students — and LITS made sure the barrier to entry was as low as possible. LITS members competed for FREE, while non-LITS IT students could join for just ₱40. Registration was open until October 5, 2025, giving teams enough time to lock in their rosters and prepare their strategies.

"Don't miss the chance to play, compete, and conquer — this is your game, your team, your legacy. CTRL + PLAY starts now!"

— #LITSEsports2025 #LITS2526 #ITuloyLang

THE CHAMPIONS

After two days of intense matches, clutch plays, and nail-biting finishes, one team rose above all others. The Azure Dragons 🐉 claimed the championship — earning not just the prize, but the bragging rights that come with being the first LITS Esports 2025 champions. Their coordination, communication, and composure under pressure set them apart from the competition from the very first round.

🥇

// WHY ESPORTS MATTERS IN IT

Beyond the competition itself, CTRL + PLAY was a reminder of something important: the skills that make a great gamer — strategic thinking, teamwork, split-second decision making, adaptability under pressure — are the same skills that make a great IT professional. The tournament celebrated not just gaming, but the mindset that LITS wants every member to carry into their careers.

To everyone who competed, watched, cheered, and organized — this event was yours. And if you didn't make it this year, start building your team now. CTRL + PLAY will be back.
