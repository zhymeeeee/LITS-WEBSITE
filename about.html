About LITS

Who we are, what we stand for, and why it matters.

// 01 — GROUND RULES

Our Ground Rules

These are the non-negotiable principles every LITS member commits to upon joining. They define how we treat one another, carry ourselves as a body, and what it truly means to wear the LITS name.

🤝
Respect & Integrity
Every member treats fellow members, advisers, and partners with dignity and professionalism — inside and outside org activities, online and in person.
📋
Accountability
Officers and members own their commitments. Absences, missed tasks, and misconduct are addressed through the org's established disciplinary process.
🔒
Confidentiality
Internal deliberations, financial records, and member information stay within the organization unless officially authorized for public release.
🌐
Inclusivity
LITS welcomes every IT student regardless of year level, background, or skill. Discrimination of any form is not tolerated in this community.
⚡
Active Participation
Membership in LITS is active, not passive. Members are expected to show up, contribute meaningfully, and engage with the community they are part of.
🏛️
Policy Compliance
All org activities align with university policies, student organization guidelines, and the LITS Constitution and By-Laws without exception.
// 02 — OBJECTIVES

Our Objectives

01
To unify all IT students under one active organization that champions their academic, professional, and personal growth throughout their university journey.
02
To provide relevant technical programs — workshops, bootcamps, and hackathons — that build skills beyond the classroom and meet real industry demand.
03
To build meaningful industry connections by cultivating partnerships with tech companies that offer internships, mentorships, and exclusive opportunities for members.
04
To foster a culture of excellence and service, where academic performance and community involvement are valued and celebrated in equal measure.
05
To represent the IT student body in inter-organizational, inter-school, and national platforms, amplifying the voice of Filipino IT students.
06
To uphold the founding values of LITS — innovation, integrity, and service — and pass them faithfully from one generation of officers to the next.
// 03 — GOALS

Our Goals

🚀
// TECHNICAL EXCELLENCE
Build Industry-Ready Members
Graduate LITS members who are professionally equipped — with certifications, real project portfolios, and industry exposure gained through org programs before they even step into a boardroom.
🤝
// COMMUNITY
Grow a Thriving IT Community
Expand LITS so that no IT student goes through university without the mentorship, camaraderie, and genuine support that LITS provides to every member of its community.
🌐
// PARTNERSHIPS
Deepen Industry Ties
Grow the partner network to deliver exclusive internships, software access, and mentorship pipelines that give LITS members a concrete advantage when they enter the industry.
💡
// IMPACT
Technology for Social Good
Channel members' IT skills into community outreach — digital literacy drives, tech solutions for NGOs, and social programs — so that LITS's reach extends well beyond the university gates.
// 04 — WHAT LITS IS FOR

What LITS Is For

// FOR BELONGING
Building a Kind Community
LITS exists first and foremost as a place where IT students feel they belong. Not just a name on a roster, not just a seat in a seminar — but a genuine community where people know each other, look out for each other, and grow together.

We believe that the environment a student grows up in shapes the professional they become. When that environment is warm, welcoming, and honest, it produces people who carry those qualities into their careers. That is the community LITS is committed to building — one where no one is left behind, every voice is heard, and every member matters.

// FOR GROWTH
Improving Students & Officers
Every program LITS runs — every bootcamp, every seminar, every competition — is designed with one intention: to make every person in this organization measurably better than when they started.

For students, that means gaining technical depth, professional confidence, and a network that opens doors. For officers, it means developing the kind of leadership that doesn't just manage a position but genuinely lifts the people around them. LITS is a training ground as much as it is an organization — and the growth that happens here follows members long after graduation.

// FOR THE FUTURE
Building Leaders for Tomorrow
The truest measure of any organization is not what its current officers achieve — it is the quality of the people they leave behind. Every LITS generation has a responsibility to invest in the next: to mentor, to document, to build systems and culture that outlast any single academic year.

LITS is a legacy organization. The leaders we shape today will go on to lead companies, build products, teach the next generation, and carry the values of LITS — integrity, service, innovation — into everything they do. This is what LITS is ultimately for: not the present, but the long arc of what our members become.

// 05 — ADVOCACY

Our Advocacy

Beyond the classrooms and competition stages, LITS goes out into the community. Our advocacy programs bring digital knowledge, human connection, and genuine service to people beyond the university walls. These are the moments that remind us why we chose technology — not just to build careers, but to build a better world for the people around us.
